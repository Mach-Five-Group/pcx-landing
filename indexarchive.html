Provisional Royalty Rate: _%

Setting PCX Royalty rate: Searching 25,000 records for comparable patents and licensing data. The closest patents are matched with classification categories to find corresponding royalty rate that is adjusted according to the ranking score to arrive at the Badge royalty rate. Once a patentee posts this Badge to the PCX site, patentee agrees that the Badge royalty rate is a reasonable royalty rate under 35 USC 284 and shall be the basis for a royalty rate for a license agreement(s) or for an option to enter a license agreement (Patent Contracts). USPTO has not issued this Patent Badge. Parties may challenge the PCX royalty rate by appeal.
